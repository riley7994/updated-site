welcome to "infomax" a one stop research website for any and all concieveable research purposes. research is defined as 'the systematic investigation into and study of materials and sources in order to establish facts and reach new conclusions.' Reasearch is a necessesary component when attempting any endeavor. Although it may seem tedious at times, the more information you have regarding a particular issue, the better equipped you are to tackle an issue from multiple angles. Knowledge itself is power, this website is an attempt to make readily avilible research information and data that will help you tackle any problem from multiple angles. statistics, graphs, and similar research websites will be made availible accross a broad spectrum of subjects.
